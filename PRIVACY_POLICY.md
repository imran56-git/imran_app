1. Introduction
Welcome to "Find Your Best Teacher Today". This app connects students with qualified teachers based on location, subjects, and experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data: Name, email, profile info, interests

Location: Your live GPS location to find nearby teachers

Messages: Chat data between students and teachers

Device Info: OS version, device ID for improving user experience


3. How We Use Your Information

To provide personalized teacher suggestions

To enable chat and communication

To improve app services and notify users


4. Third-Party Services Used
We integrate the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google) – Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Messaging

Google Maps SDK & Location API – For displaying maps and live location

Google Cloud Storage – For storing profile images and other media

Emoji Picker & Audio Recorder Libraries – For enhanced chat experience


These services may collect anonymized usage data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing & Security
Your personal data is never sold. We only share necessary information with trusted services mentioned above to operate the app. All data is encrypted and securely stored.

6. User Rights
You may access, edit, or delete your profile anytime.
You can request full data deletion by contacting us via email.

7. Children’s Policy
Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Policy Updates
This privacy policy may change. Any updates will be shared within the app or via email.
